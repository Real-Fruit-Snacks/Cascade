# Morning Smoothie

A quick, energizing smoothie to start the day right. #recipe #health

---

## Ingredients

- 1 frozen banana
- 1/2 cup frozen blueberries
- 1/2 cup frozen mango chunks
- 1 cup almond milk (unsweetened)
- 1 tbsp almond butter
- 1 tsp chia seeds
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- Small handful of spinach (you won't taste it)

---

## Instructions

1. Add the almond milk to the blender first — this helps everything blend smoothly
2. Add spinach and blend briefly until liquid
3. Add the remaining ingredients
4. Blend on high for 45–60 seconds until completely smooth
5. Pour and drink immediately for best texture

---

## Variations

**Green Protein Boost** — swap mango for 1 scoop vanilla protein powder and add kale instead of spinach

**Berry Antioxidant** — use all berries (blueberry, strawberry, raspberry), skip mango, add a squeeze of lemon

**Tropical** — use mango, pineapple, coconut milk instead of almond milk, skip turmeric

---

## Nutrition Notes

- Roughly 350 calories as written
- Good source of fiber, healthy fats, and antioxidants
- The turmeric + black pepper combo (add a pinch) boosts anti-inflammatory absorption
- Prep tip: freeze banana slices and portion other frozen fruit into bags the night before for a 60-second morning routine
