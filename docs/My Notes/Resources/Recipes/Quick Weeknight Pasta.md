# Quick Weeknight Pasta

Ready in 20 minutes. Works with whatever's in the pantry. #recipe

---

## Ingredients

- 300g pasta (rigatoni or penne works best)
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
- Salt and black pepper to taste
- Fresh basil or flat-leaf parsley
- Parmesan to finish

---

## Steps

1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil
2. Cook pasta according to package directions; reserve 1/2 cup pasta water before draining
3. While pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a wide pan over medium heat
4. Add garlic and red pepper flakes; cook 60 seconds until fragrant (don't let it brown)
5. Pour in crushed tomatoes, season with salt and pepper, simmer 8–10 minutes
6. Add drained pasta to the sauce; toss to coat, adding pasta water a splash at a time to loosen
7. Finish with torn basil and a generous handful of Parmesan

---

## Tips

- The pasta water is the secret — the starch binds the sauce to the noodles
- A pinch of sugar balances overly acidic canned tomatoes
- Cook garlic low and slow; burnt garlic ruins the whole dish

---

## Variations

**Arrabiata** — double the red pepper flakes, skip the basil, add a splash of red wine to the sauce

**With Greens** — stir in a handful of baby spinach or kale in the last minute of cooking

**Pantry Protein** — add a drained can of white beans or chickpeas with the tomatoes
