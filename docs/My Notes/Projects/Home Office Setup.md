## Overview

The current setup is functional but not great for long coding sessions. The monitor is too low, the chair is a dining chair, and the lighting gives me a headache by 3 PM. This project is about making the space support deep work — not aesthetics for its own sake, though I wouldn't complain if it looked good.

- [[Health & Fitness]] has notes on proper monitor height and posture (eyes level with top third of screen, arm's reach away)

### Ergonomics Checklist (from research)

- [ ] Monitor top at or slightly below eye level
- [ ] Elbows at ~90° when typing
- [ ] Feet flat on floor (or footrest)
- [ ] Screen 50–70 cm from eyes
- [ ] Bias lighting behind monitor to reduce contrast fatigue
