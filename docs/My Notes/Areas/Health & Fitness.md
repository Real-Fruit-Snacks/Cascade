# Health & Fitness

Ongoing tracking of physical health, exercise habits, and nutrition. The goal is consistency over intensity — small daily actions compound over time. See also [[Atomic Habits]] for the framework behind this.

## Current Goals

- Run a 5K without stopping by end of Q2
- Average 7.5+ hours of sleep per night
- Reduce caffeine dependency (max 1 coffee before noon)
- Hit 10,000 steps on at least 5 days per week
- Build a morning routine that sticks — connected to [[Home Office Setup]] ergonomics

## Weekly Routine

| Day       | Exercise              | Duration | Notes                    |
| --------- | --------------------- | -------- | ------------------------ |
| Monday    | Strength (upper body) | 45 min   | Push/pull split          |
| Tuesday   | Run / walk            | 30 min   | Easy pace, Zone 2        |
| Wednesday | Rest or yoga          | 20 min   | Stretch focus            |
| Thursday  | Strength (lower body) | 45 min   | Squats, deadlifts        |
| Friday    | Run                   | 35 min   | Slightly faster pace     |
| Saturday  | Long walk or hike     | 60 min   | Outdoors when possible   |
| Sunday    | Full rest             | —        | Recovery, no screen time |

## Nutrition Notes

Trying to keep things simple rather than obsessive. A few principles that are working:

- **Protein first** — aim for 30g protein at breakfast (usually eggs or Greek yogurt)
- **Limit ultra-processed food** — not zero, just not the default
- **Hydration** — 2L water before any coffee or tea
- Prepping the [[Morning Smoothie]] on weekdays saves decision fatigue

### Rough Macro Targets (maintenance)

| Macro       | Daily Target |
| ----------- | ------------ |
| Protein     | 150g         |
| Carbs       | 200–250g     |
| Fat         | 70–90g       |
| Calories    | ~2,400       |
