# Writing

Writing is thinking made visible. This area tracks writing practice, ongoing projects, and the slow accumulation of craft. Discipline here compounds — see [[Deep Work]] for why protected writing time matters more than inspiration.

- Finish the short story draft by end of Q2
- Improve at: scene-setting, cutting filler, strong opening sentences

- What does a city sound like at 4am to someone who can't sleep?
- The first week after a major technological shift — mundane details

- What would you write if no one would ever read it?

## Style Notes

Things I'm actively working on:

- **Cut the preamble** — most first paragraphs can be deleted. Start in the middle.
- **Concrete over abstract** — "a red folder on a cluttered desk" beats "disorganization"
- **Read aloud** — if you stumble, the sentence needs work
- **One idea per paragraph** — if you can't summarize it in a sentence, split it
- **Verbs carry weight** — prefer active, specific verbs over adverb-heavy ones
